Filters

Ctrl+Shift+x: toggles Dark Mode

A - Decreases speed by 0.25.

S - Increases speed by 0.25.

Q - Decreases saturation.

W - Increases saturation.

E - Decreases contrast.

R - Increases contrast.

[ - Decreases Brightness

] - Increases Brightness

x - Inverts video.

This is just a prototype for testing, the fully functional extension will be released soon❄️
